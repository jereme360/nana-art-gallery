About the Artist

Nancy Ziegler is a lifelong artist inspired by nature, the vibrancy of life, and the beauty of the subjects she paints. Her paintings invite viewers to pause and appreciate the world around them.

Working primarily in oils, Nancy continues to explore new techniques while staying true to her artistic vision. Each piece tells a story, reflecting decades of dedication to her craft.
